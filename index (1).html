Developmental Milestones — 0 to 2 Years

0–6 Months
  • Smiles at people
  • Recognizes parents
  • Holds head up
  • Rolls over
  • Starts babbling
  • Reaches for toys
6–12 Months
  • Sits without support
  • Crawls
  • Pulls to stand
  • Says simple words ("mama", "dada")
  • Picks up small objects
12–24 Months
  • Walks independently
  • Runs and climbs
  • Uses 20–50 words
  • Follows simple instructions
  • Points to body parts

Common Problems & Symptoms — 0 to 2 Years

🌡️ Fever
Symptoms
  • Temperature above 38°C
  • Irritability
  • Reduced feeding
What to do
  • Keep hydrated
  • Light clothing
  • Doctor-approved fever medicine
🤧 Cold & Cough
Symptoms
  • Runny nose
  • Sneezing
  • Mild cough
What to do
  • Saline nasal drops
  • Plenty of fluids
  • Humidified air
💧 Diarrhea
Symptoms
  • Loose stools
  • Dehydration signs
What to do
  • Give ORS
  • Continue feeding
  • Seek medical help if severe
🦷 Teething
Symptoms
  • Drooling
  • Chewing objects
  • Irritability
What to do
  • Teething rings
  • Gentle gum massage

🚨 Emergency Warning Signs — All Ages

Seek urgent medical help if a child has any of the following:

Difficulty breathing
Blue lips
Seizures
Severe dehydration
Unconsciousness
High fever in young infants
Persistent vomiting
Severe allergic reaction
